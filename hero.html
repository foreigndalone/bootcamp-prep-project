Hero of Middle-earth

Though not a king or a warrior in the usual sense, Gandalf was the spark that united elves, men, dwarves, and hobbits against the darkness. His true heroism lay in inspiring others to rise above fear.

Voices About Gandalf

Humility — Chose a simple life, hiding his immense power under the guise of an old wanderer.

Courage — Faced enemies greater than himself, including the Balrog and Sauron’s forces.

Wisdom — He guided others with knowledge of ages past.

Leadership — United diverse peoples to stand together when all seemed lost.

Compassion — Believed in the small and overlooked, especially hobbits.
