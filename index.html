Gandalf the Grey

A wandering wizard, a guide, and a guardian of Middle-earth.

A Brief Life of Gandalf

  • Before the Third Age — Sent by the Valar into Middle-earth in the form of an old man, as one of the five Istari.
  • Third Age, 1000 — Arrives in Middle-earth, known as Gandalf the Grey.
  • Third Age, 2941 — Helps Thorin Oakenshield and his company of dwarves on the Quest of Erebor.
  • Third Age, 3018 — Warns Frodo about the true nature of the One Ring.
  • Third Age, 3019 — Falls in battle with the Balrog of Moria, returns as Gandalf the White.
  • Third Age, 3019–3021 — Leads the Free Peoples to victory against Sauron and sails to the Undying Lands.
